## docker-compose.yml
```yml
---
services:
  pigallery2:
    image: bpatrik/pigallery2:latest
    container_name: pigallery2
    environment:
      - NODE_ENV=production
    volumes:
      - ./db:/app/data/db
      - ./data/config:/app/data/config
      - ./data/tmp:/app/data/tmp
      - ./data/images:/app/data/images
    ports:
      - 3000:80
    restart: unless-stopped
```
