## docker-compose.yml
```yml
---
services:
  openproject:
    image: openproject/community
    container_name: openproject
    restart: unless-stopped
    environment:
      - SECRET_KEY_BASE=secret_key
    ports:
      - "3090:80"
    volumes:
      - ./pgdata:/var/openproject/pgdata
      - ./assets:/var/openproject/assets
```
