## docker-compose.yml
```yml
services:
  bluespice:
    image: bluespice/bluespice-free
    container_name: bluespice
    restart: unless-stopped
    environment:
      - TZ=Europe/Dublin
      - bs_lang=en
      - bs_url=https://bluespice.example.com
    ports:
      - 3123:80
    volumes:
      - ./data:/data
```
